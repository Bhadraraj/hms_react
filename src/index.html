OUR STORY

At Shri Vidhya Mandhir children will have the freedom of learning according to their wish and taste, so that they will become independent learners.

The approach to education is inquiry-based, collaborative and Project-based learning with interdisciplinary and multidisciplinary aspects reflecting global perception in school learning.

Beyond the curriculum we impart holistic development of the children to mould them to be accomplished human beings. Conducive atmosphere at our school prepares the children to face challenges in 21st century learning and makes them creators.

To provide quality education in a safe environment, conducive to learning. To implement successful programs that produce lasting results and be one of the finest schools in our nation producing respectful citizens to uphold the honour and the dignity of our motherland.

To reach out technology-based holistic education by recruiting dedicated and highly motivated professionals. The approach to education is inquiry-based, collaborative with inter-disciplinary and multi-disciplinary aspects that reflect global perception in school learning.
